##############
CMake Language
##############

If you are thinking about developing for CMakePP odds are you already are
familiar with the basics of the CMake language. What you may not be so familiar
with are the nuances of the CMake language (and there are many). The chapters in
this part are designed to familiarize you with these nuances and introduce the
CMake best-practices to avoid them.
